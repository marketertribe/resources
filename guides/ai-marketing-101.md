# Getting Started with AI Marketing

A practical guide for marketers who want to use AI to work smarter, not harder.

## The AI Marketing Stack

### Tier 1: Free Tools (Start Here)
- **ChatGPT Free / Claude Free**: Copy, brainstorming, strategy
- **Canva (Free tier)**: Design with AI-powered tools
- **Google Analytics 4**: AI-powered insights
- **HubSpot CRM Free**: Contact management

### Tier 2: Essential Paid Tools ($50-200/mo)
- **ChatGPT Plus / Claude Pro**: Faster, smarter AI assistance
- **Jasper or Copy.ai**: Dedicated marketing copy AI
- **Midjourney or DALL-E**: Image generation for ads and social
- **Zapier / Make**: Automation workflows

### Tier 3: Advanced ($200+/mo)
- **Custom GPTs / Claude Projects**: Trained on your brand voice
- **Surfer SEO or Clearscope**: AI-powered SEO content
- **Synthesia or HeyGen**: AI video creation

## 5 Quick Wins with AI

### 1. Email Subject Line Testing
Generate 20 subject line variations in 30 seconds. Test 4-5 per campaign.

### 2. Social Media Repurposing
Turn one blog post into 10 social media posts, 3 email snippets, and 1 video script.

### 3. Ad Copy Variations
Generate 10 Facebook ad variations from one winning angle. Test in batches of 3.

### 4. Customer Research
Paste reviews and support tickets into AI. Ask: "What are the top 5 pain points and desires?"

### 5. Content Calendar
Give AI your niche, audience, and goals. Generate a month of content ideas in 5 minutes.

## The Golden Rule

**AI is your co-pilot, not your pilot.** Use it to generate drafts, brainstorm ideas, and handle repetitive tasks. Always review, edit, and add your voice before publishing.
