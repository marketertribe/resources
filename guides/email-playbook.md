# The Email Marketing Playbook

Everything you need to build a profitable email marketing system.

## The Email Foundation

### List Building Essentials
1. **One clear lead magnet** per audience segment
2. **Opt-in forms** on every page (exit intent, inline, sidebar)
3. **Welcome sequence** that delivers value and builds trust
4. **Regular sending cadence** (minimum 1x/week)

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | Good | Great | Excellent |
|--------|------|-------|-----------|
| Open rate | 20%+ | 30%+ | 40%+ |
| Click rate | 2%+ | 4%+ | 6%+ |
| Unsubscribe | <0.5% | <0.3% | <0.1% |
| Revenue/email | $0.05+ | $0.10+ | $0.25+ |

## The 5 Emails Every Business Needs

1. **Welcome Email**: Deliver the promise, set expectations
2. **Value Email**: One actionable tip or insight
3. **Story Email**: Personal story that connects to your offer
4. **Social Proof Email**: Customer results and testimonials
5. **Pitch Email**: Clear offer with a reason to act now

## Subject Line Formulas That Work

- **Curiosity**: "The one thing I'd change if I started over"
- **How-to**: "How to [result] without [pain point]"
- **Numbers**: "3 mistakes killing your [thing]"
- **Personal**: "I messed this up (and what I learned)"
- **Direct**: "[First name], quick question"

## Segmentation Strategy

### Segment by:
- **Engagement**: Active (opened in 30 days), Warm (30-60), Cold (60+)
- **Purchase history**: Non-buyers, one-time, repeat
- **Interest**: Based on which links they click
- **Source**: Where they signed up

### Send differently to each:
- Active → Full content + offers
- Warm → Re-engagement + best content
- Cold → Win-back sequence → Sunset
