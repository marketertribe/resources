# Landing Page Frameworks

Proven structures for high-converting landing pages.

## The Classic Opt-In Page

```
[HEADLINE: Benefit-driven, 5-10 words]
[SUBHEADLINE: Expand on the benefit, mention target audience]

[HERO IMAGE or MOCKUP of lead magnet]

What you'll learn:
• [Specific benefit 1]
• [Specific benefit 2]
• [Specific benefit 3]

[EMAIL INPUT]
[CTA BUTTON: Action verb + benefit]

[SOCIAL PROOF: "Join 500+ marketers" or testimonial]
```

## The Long-Form Sales Page (PASTOR Framework)

```
P — Problem: Open with the pain your audience feels
A — Amplify: Make the problem feel urgent
S — Story: Tell a relatable story (yours or a customer's)
T — Transformation: Paint the before/after picture
O — Offer: Present your solution with everything included
R — Response: Clear CTA with urgency
```

### Section Breakdown:

1. **Hero**: Headline + subheadline + CTA
2. **Problem**: 3 pain points your audience recognizes
3. **Failed solutions**: What they've already tried
4. **Solution intro**: Your product/service
5. **Benefits**: Feature → Benefit → Transformation
6. **Social proof**: Testimonials, logos, numbers
7. **What's included**: Detailed breakdown
8. **Pricing**: Clear, with anchor pricing if applicable
9. **FAQ**: Address top 5 objections
10. **Final CTA**: Urgency + risk reversal (guarantee)
