# Social Media Content Calendar Template

A weekly posting framework for consistent social media marketing.

## Weekly Content Pillars

| Day | Content Type | Platform Focus | Goal |
|-----|-------------|----------------|------|
| Monday | Educational tip | LinkedIn, X | Build authority |
| Tuesday | Behind the scenes | Instagram, Facebook | Build trust |
| Wednesday | Curated content / share | X, LinkedIn | Provide value |
| Thursday | Engagement post (question/poll) | All platforms | Drive interaction |
| Friday | Win / case study | LinkedIn, Facebook | Social proof |
| Saturday | Personal story / lesson | Instagram, X | Build connection |
| Sunday | Week ahead / planning | Stories | Stay top of mind |

## Content Ratios

- **50% Value**: Tips, how-tos, educational content
- **20% Community**: Questions, replies, shares, engagement
- **20% Personal**: Stories, lessons, behind the scenes
- **10% Promotion**: Products, offers, CTAs

## Platform-Specific Notes

### X/Twitter
- Post 2-5x daily
- Mix threads (2x/week) with single tweets
- Reply to 10+ people in your niche daily
- Best times: 8-10am, 12-1pm, 5-7pm

### LinkedIn
- Post 3-5x weekly
- Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Best times: 7-9am, 11am-12pm weekdays

### Instagram
- Feed: 3-4x weekly
- Stories: Daily
- Reels: 2-3x weekly
- Carousels get the highest saves

### Facebook
- Post 3-5x weekly
- Groups > Pages for engagement
- Video and image posts outperform text
